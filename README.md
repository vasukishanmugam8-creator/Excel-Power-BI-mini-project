## 💡 Key Insights

- Credit card transaction values are higher in certain banks (e.g., HDFC, ICICI) while debit card usage dominates in public sector banks. 
- Digital spend shows a steady upward trend over time, indicating customer preference for online and QR-based payments.
- Physical transactions (ATM withdrawals) are significantly lower compared to digital transactions (POS, QR, UPI). 

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Banks should invest more in digital infrastructure and customer awareness campaigns to accelerate digital adoption.
- Encourage cashless transactions by offering rewards/discounts for UPI and card payments.
- Public sector banks should focus on bridging the digital gap by expanding QR/UPI services in semi-urban and rural areas.
